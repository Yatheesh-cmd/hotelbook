Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in hospitality

Founded in 2010 with a Vision

Hotelfire began as a small team of travel enthusiasts who believed hotel booking should be simple, transparent, and rewarding. What started as a passion project has grown into a platform serving millions of travelers annually.

Our founders, seasoned travelers themselves, recognized the need for a booking service that prioritized customer experience over profits. This people-first approach remains at our core today.
